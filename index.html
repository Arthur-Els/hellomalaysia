Inspiring Figure

Datuk Nicol David

World-Class Malaysian Athlete

Nicol David is one of Malaysia's most inspiring figures. She dominated world squash for years and became a symbol of discipline, consistency, and national pride.

  • 8-time World Open Champion
  • Former World No. 1
  • Global ambassador for youth and sports
